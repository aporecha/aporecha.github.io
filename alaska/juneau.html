Glacier Salt Cave & Spa

Glacier Salt Cave & Spa is the only salt spa located in Alaska, and it is one of the most popular spas in Juneau. At this spa, patrons are able to spend an hour relaxing in one of the Himalayan treatment rooms that are filled with salt crystals. Sometimes referred to as halo therapy, this holistic treatment may provide people with a variety of health benefits. People with a variety of health conditions, including allergies, asthma or chronic skin conditions, report improvement after halo therapy.
