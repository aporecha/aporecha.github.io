Summary

New Jersey, known as the Garden State, offers a diverse mix of attractions, stunning scenery, and delicious cuisine.

Heres an overview of what to expect:

Top Attractions

  1. Atlantic City: Famous for its casinos, boardwalk, and beaches, Atlantic City is a hub of entertainment and nightlife.
  2. Liberty State Park: Located in Jersey City, this park provides stunning views of the Manhattan skyline and is home to the Liberty Science Center and the Statue of Liberty ferry.
  3. Cape May: A charming Victorian seaside town, known for its historic architecture, beautiful beaches, and boutique shopping.
  4. Princeton University: Visit this Ivy League campus for its stunning architecture, art museum, and lush gardens.
  5. The Delaware Water Gap National Recreation Area: Ideal for hiking, kayaking, and enjoying scenic views along the Delaware River.

Scenery

  • Pine Barrens: This vast forested area is known for its unique ecosystem, including beautiful pines, bogs, and rare wildlife.
  • Skylands Region: Home to the picturesque Ringwood State Park and High Point State Park, which offers stunning vistas and hiking trails.
  • Barnegat Bay: Great for kayaking, fishing, and enjoying the coastal scenery.

Food and Drink

  • Taylor Ham (Pork Roll): A New Jersey breakfast staple, typically served on a bagel or with eggs.
  • Cheesesteaks: While often associated with Philadelphia, New Jersey has its own take on this beloved sandwich.
  • Italian Dishes: New Jersey is home to numerous Italian restaurants, featuring classic dishes like lasagna, cannoli, and pizza.
  • Craft Beer: The state has a vibrant craft beer scene, with numerous breweries offering tastings and tours.
  • Wine Trails: Explore the wine regions of the state, particularly in the Cape May and Warren County areas, where you can enjoy local tastings.

Places to Visit

  • Grounds for Sculpture: An outdoor sculpture park and museum in Hamilton, showcasing contemporary art in a beautifully landscaped setting.
  • Red Bank: Known for its arts scene, boutiques, and restaurants, its a great place to explore culture and cuisine.
  • The Thomas Edison National Historical Park: Located in West Orange, this park includes Edisons laboratory and home, showcasing his inventions.
  • Six Flags Great Adventure: A popular amusement park offering thrilling rides and entertainment for all ages.

Outdoor Activities

  • Hiking and Biking: Explore the Appalachian Trail or the numerous parks and trails throughout the state.
  • Beaches: From the sandy shores of Wildwood to the family-friendly beaches of Long Beach Island, there's a stretch of coastline for everyone.
